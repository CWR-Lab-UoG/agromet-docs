## Scientific Literature

### Flux-Gradient Method

Baldocchi, D.D., Hicks, B.B., & Meyers, T.P. (1988). Measuring biosphere-atmosphere exchanges of biologically related gases with micrometeorological methods. *Ecology*, 69(5), 1331-1340.

Meyers, T.P., et al. (1996). A comparison of summertime water and CO₂ fluxes over rangeland for well watered and drought conditions. *Agricultural and Forest Meteorology*, 89, 87-105.

Zhao, J., et al. (2019). An evaluation of the flux-gradient and the eddy covariance method to measure CH₄, CO₂, and H₂O fluxes from small ponds. *Agricultural and Forest Meteorology*, 275, 255-264.

### Monin-Obukhov Similarity Theory

Monin, A.S., & Obukhov, A.M. (1954). Basic laws of turbulent mixing in the surface layer of the atmosphere. *Tr. Akad. Nauk SSSR Geophiz. Inst*, 24, 163-187.

Businger, J.A., et al. (1971). Flux-profile relationships in the atmospheric surface layer. *Journal of Atmospheric Sciences*, 28, 181-189.

Högström, U. (1988). Non-dimensional wind and temperature profiles in the atmospheric surface layer: A re-evaluation. *Boundary-Layer Meteorology*, 42, 55-78.

Foken, T. (2006). 50 years of the Monin-Obukhov similarity theory. *Boundary-Layer Meteorology*, 119, 431-447.

Paulson, C.A. (1970). The mathematical representation of wind speed and temperature profiles in the unstable atmospheric surface layer. *Journal of Applied Meteorology*, 9, 857-861.

### Eddy Covariance Method

Baldocchi, D.D. (2014). Measuring fluxes of trace gases and energy between ecosystems and the atmosphere - the state and future of the eddy covariance method. *Global Change Biology*, 20, 3600-3609.

Webb, E.K., Pearman, G.I., & Leuning, R. (1980). Correction of flux measurements for density effects due to heat and water vapour transfer. *Quarterly Journal of the Royal Meteorological Society*, 106, 85-100.

Moncrieff, J.B., et al. (1997). A system to measure surface fluxes of momentum, sensible heat, water vapour and carbon dioxide. *Journal of Hydrology*, 188-189, 589-611.

### Data Processing and Quality Control

Mahrt, L. (1998). Flux sampling errors for aircraft and towers. *Journal of Atmospheric and Oceanic Technology*, 15, 416-429.

Griffis, T.J., et al. (2003). Ecophysiological controls on the carbon balances of three southern boreal forests. *Agricultural and Forest Meteorology*, 117, 53-71.

Foken, T., & Wichura, B. (1996). Tools for quality assessment of surface-based flux measurements. *Agricultural and Forest Meteorology*, 78, 83-105.


### Turbulence and Boundary Layer

Kaimal, J.C., & Finnigan, J.J. (1994). *Atmospheric Boundary Layer Flows: Their Structure and Measurement*. Oxford University Press.

Stull, R.B. (1988). *An Introduction to Boundary Layer Meteorology*. Kluwer Academic Publishers.
